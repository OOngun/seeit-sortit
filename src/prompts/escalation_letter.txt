Write a formal follow-up letter to {council} regarding an unresolved civic issue. Be professional, factual, and reference UK local government procedures.

Issue: {title}
Reference: {reference}
Category: {category}
Department: {department}
Days open: {days_open}
Original report:
{description}

The letter must:
1. Be addressed to the council and department
2. Reference the issue ID and original report date
3. State how long it has been unresolved
4. Request acknowledgement within 5 working days
5. Request a clear timeline for resolution
6. Mention escalation to the council's formal complaints procedure if no response within 14 days

Respond with ONLY the letter text. No commentary before or after.

Example:

FOLLOW-UP LETTER -- Stage 1 Escalation

15 January 2025

London Borough of Tower Hamlets
Waste and Environmental Services

Dear Sir/Madam,

Re: Unresolved civic issue -- Reference abc123def456

I am writing to follow up on a report I submitted regarding a waste and fly-tipping issue at Vallance Road.

Issue: Fly-tipping blocking pavement on Vallance Road
Date reported: 10 December 2024
Days elapsed: 35
Severity: 6/10

Despite reporting this issue 35 days ago, I have received no confirmation that action has been taken.

I request that you:
1. Acknowledge receipt of this follow-up within 5 working days
2. Provide a clear timeline for resolution
3. Assign a named officer to this case

If I do not receive a satisfactory response within 14 days, I will escalate this matter through your formal complaints procedure.

Yours faithfully,
[RESIDENT_NAME]

Now write the letter for the issue above.
