# Priority MP Targets for Outreach

Shortlist of London MPs with civic tech, digital government, or local infrastructure backgrounds. These are the highest-conversion cold-outreach targets — they're more likely to actually read and respond.

## Tier 1 — Strongest fit

### Joe Powell — Kensington and Bayswater (Labour)
- **Background**: former CEO of the Open Government Partnership
- **Why**: explicit civic tech / transparency credentials. Strongest single target for a citizen-empowering platform.
- **Borough**: Kensington and Chelsea + Westminster (cross-borough)

### Georgia Gould — Queen's Park and Maida Vale (Labour)
- **Background**: former Leader of Camden Council
- **Why**: deep local government experience, including digital transformation of council services. She'll grok our value proposition immediately.
- **Borough**: Westminster + Brent

### Feryal Clark — Enfield North (Labour)
- **Background**: AI/digital government minister at DSIT (Department for Science, Innovation and Technology)
- **Why**: literally responsible for AI in government. NVIDIA hackathon + local AI inference = directly in her brief.
- **Borough**: Enfield

## Tier 2 — Strong fit

### Meg Hillier — Hackney South and Shoreditch (Labour)
- **Background**: former Chair of the Public Accounts Committee (PAC) — scrutinised government digital programmes for years
- **Why**: knows how badly councils struggle with digital. Pragmatic, not anti-AI.
- **Borough**: Hackney

### James Murray — Ealing North (Labour)
- **Background**: Exchequer Secretary to the Treasury
- **Why**: budget angle. Our pitch: "councils waste money on duplicate reports — we cut admin overhead."
- **Borough**: Ealing

### Liam Conlon — Beckenham and Penge (Labour)
- **Background**: digital/local-services interest
- **Why**: newer MP, more receptive to outside ideas. Multi-borough constituency = good pilot candidate.
- **Borough**: Bromley + Lewisham

### Rupa Huq — Ealing Central and Acton (Labour)
- **Background**: active in all-party parliamentary groups on tech
- **Why**: tech-curious, accessible.
- **Borough**: Ealing

## Outreach Strategy

**Round 1** (high-touch, personalised): Tier 1 MPs. Send personalised emails referencing their specific background. Maximum 6 emails.

**Round 2** (bulk): All 75 London MPs with the standard outreach email. Send via mail-merge or BCC batching. Expect 2-5% response rate.

**Round 3** (follow-up): MPs who opened but didn't reply (if we have tracking) get one follow-up after 7 days.

**Don't**: Spam the same MP multiple times. Don't ask them to do anything immediately — first email is just intro + offer of a pilot conversation.
