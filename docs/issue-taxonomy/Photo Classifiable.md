# Photo Classifiable

Issues that can be identified and classified from a photo alone. These are the primary targets for our vision model (Qwen2.5-VL-7B or Llama 4 Scout).

## Full Support (high confidence from photo)
- [[Roads and Highways]] — potholes, cracked paving, damaged signs
- [[Waste and Fly-Tipping]] — dumped rubbish, overflowing bins
- [[Street Cleanliness]] — graffiti, dog fouling, litter
- [[Pavements and Footways]] — trip hazards, damaged slabs
- [[Vehicles]] — abandoned cars, bikes, e-scooters
- [[Trees and Vegetation]] — fallen trees, dangerous branches
- [[Parks and Public Spaces]] — broken equipment, damaged furniture
- [[Dead Animals]]
- [[Planning and Building]] — visible unauthorised works

## Partial Support (photo helps but needs description)
- [[Street Lighting and Traffic]] — can see damaged fixture, but "not working" needs description
- [[Antisocial Behaviour]] — evidence (needles, vandalism) but not the behaviour itself
- [[Housing]] — exterior damage, damp/mould visible
- [[Utilities]] — burst pipe, damaged cover visible
- [[Pollution]] — smoke, discoloured water sometimes visible

## Not Photo-Based
- [[Noise and Nuisance]]
- [[Food and Business]] (mostly)
- [[Fraud and Misuse]] (mostly)
- [[Pest Control]] (sometimes)

## Coverage
13 of 18 categories have at least partial photo support.
These 13 categories cover approximately **75% of all civic reports by volume**.
