# Dead Animals

**Photo classifiable:** Yes
**Time sensitive:** Yes (health hazard, road obstruction)
**Responsible authority:** Borough street cleaning / highways

## Issue Types
- Dead animal on road or public land
- Dead wildlife in waterways

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Blocking road or pavement → urgent
- Large animal (deer, fox) vs small (pigeon)
- In waterway → potential contamination
- Near school, playground, food premises

## Routes To
- Borough street cleaning (public land)
- Borough highways (on road)
- Environment Agency (waterways)
- RSPCA (if animal appears to have been harmed)
