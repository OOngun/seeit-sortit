# Utilities

**Photo classifiable:** Partially (burst pipe, damaged cover)
**Time sensitive:** Yes (gas, electricity, water)
**Responsible authority:** Utility companies (council often first point of contact)

## Issue Types
- Water leak / burst main
- Gas smell
- Exposed wiring / electrical hazard
- Damaged manhole / utility cover

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Gas smell → 999 / National Gas Emergency line, highest priority
- Exposed electricity → UK Power Networks emergency
- Water leak scale (burst main vs dripping)
- Location (road, pavement, near buildings)

## Routes To
- Thames Water (water leaks in London)
- National Gas Emergency Service (gas: 0800 111 999)
- UK Power Networks (electricity in London)
- Borough highways (damaged covers, as interim safety measure)
- Openreach (telecoms infrastructure)

## Note for Our Agent
Utility issues often get reported to the council but need forwarding to the utility company. Our routing agent should detect these and route correctly — this is a common frustration point for residents.
