# Roads and Highways

**Volume share:** ~50% of all civic reports
**Weekly volume:** ~870/week (potholes alone)
**Photo classifiable:** Yes
**Time sensitive:** Some (flooding, debris)
**Responsible authority:** Borough highways department, TfL (for red routes)

## Issue Types
- Potholes / road surface damage
- Cracked or uneven paving slabs
- Blocked drains / flooding
- Roadworks obstructions
- Damaged safety barriers / railings
- Damaged or missing bollards and signs
- Road markings faded or missing
- Debris / spillage on road

## Severity Ranking Signals
- [[Roads and Highways]] issues near schools → higher severity ([[Photo Classifiable]])
- Traffic volume from DfT AADF data → busier road = higher impact
- Collision history from STATS19 → road with prior accidents = higher priority
- Road classification (A-road vs residential) from DfT data

## Routes To
- Borough highways team (most cases)
- TfL (red routes, A-roads managed by TfL)
- National Highways (motorways, strategic roads — rare in London)

## Demographic Note
More affluent areas report more potholes; more deprived areas report more [[Street Cleanliness]] issues (mySociety research).
