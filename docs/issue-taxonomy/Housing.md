# Housing

**Photo classifiable:** Partially (exterior damage, damp, mould)
**Time sensitive:** Yes (dangerous buildings, illegal eviction)
**Responsible authority:** Borough housing department

## Issue Types
- Council housing repairs
- Private rental poor conditions (damp, mould, disrepair)
- Dangerous buildings / structures
- Illegal eviction / landlord harassment
- Short-term let violations (Airbnb exceeding 90-day limit)
- HMO (house in multiple occupation) concerns
- Overcrowding

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Dangerous building → immediate
- Illegal eviction → immediate
- Damp/mould with children or vulnerable residents
- HMO without licence (fire safety risk)

## Routes To
- Borough housing department (council tenants)
- Borough environmental health (private rental conditions)
- Borough planning (short-term let violations, HMO licensing)
- Police (illegal eviction is a criminal offence)
