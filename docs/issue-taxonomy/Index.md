# Civic Issue Taxonomy

The central hub connecting all reportable issue categories.

## Categories

### Tier 1 — High Volume (~75% of reports)
- [[Roads and Highways]] — ~50% of all reports
- [[Waste and Fly-Tipping]] — largest environmental category
- [[Street Cleanliness]] — graffiti, dog fouling, litter
- [[Street Lighting and Traffic]] — safety-critical

### Tier 2 — Solid Use Cases
- [[Pavements and Footways]]
- [[Vehicles]]
- [[Trees and Vegetation]]
- [[Parks and Public Spaces]]

### Tier 3 — Long Tail
- [[Noise and Nuisance]]
- [[Pollution]]
- [[Antisocial Behaviour]]
- [[Housing]]
- [[Planning and Building]]
- [[Pest Control]]
- [[Food and Business]]
- [[Dead Animals]]
- [[Utilities]]
- [[Fraud and Misuse]]

## Cross-cutting Tags
- [[Photo Classifiable]] — 13 of 18 categories
- [[Time Sensitive]] — requires urgent response
- [[Geolocated]] — tied to a specific location (all categories)

## Intake Paths
- **Photo + Voice**: Tier 1 and Tier 2 (auto-classify from image)
- **Voice / Text only**: Noise, some pollution, food safety, fraud
