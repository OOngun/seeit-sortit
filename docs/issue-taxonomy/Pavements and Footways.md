# Pavements and Footways

**Photo classifiable:** Yes
**Time sensitive:** Yes (trip hazards)
**Responsible authority:** Borough highways

## Issue Types
- Pavement damage / cracked slabs
- Trip hazards (raised paving, tree roots)
- Blocked public footpath or bridleway
- Obstructions on pavement (A-boards, abandoned items)

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Near care homes, hospitals (elderly/disabled population)
- High footfall area (TfL station data proxy)
- Trip hazard height (>20mm is typically council intervention threshold)
- Population density

## Routes To
- Borough highways department
- Borough enforcement (obstruction by businesses)
