# Parks and Public Spaces

**Photo classifiable:** Yes
**Time sensitive:** Varies (playground damage = urgent)
**Responsible authority:** Borough parks department

## Issue Types
- Damaged park furniture (benches, fences, gates)
- Park cleanliness issues
- Playground equipment damage
- Cemetery or memorial damage
- Waterway pollution in parks
- Overgrown paths in parks
- Damaged sports facilities

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Playground equipment → child safety, highest priority
- Near school or nursery
- Park usage level (popular parks = higher impact)
- Waterway pollution → Environment Agency may also need notification

## Routes To
- Borough parks department
- Environment Agency (waterway pollution)
- Police (vandalism, if criminal damage)
