# Antisocial Behaviour

**Photo classifiable:** Partially (evidence: needles, vandalism, graffiti)
**Time sensitive:** Yes (needles, drug paraphernalia)
**Responsible authority:** Borough community safety, police

## Issue Types
- Drug use / paraphernalia in public (syringes, needles)
- Vandalism
- Harassment / intimidation
- Rough sleeping encampments
- Illegal bonfires

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Needles/syringes → immediate public health risk, highest priority
- Near playground, school, nursery
- Pattern of repeat incidents at location
- Vandalism to safety infrastructure (damaged fencing near railway, etc.)

## Routes To
- Borough community safety team
- Police (criminal behaviour)
- Outreach services (rough sleeping — not enforcement)
- Fire brigade (illegal bonfires)
