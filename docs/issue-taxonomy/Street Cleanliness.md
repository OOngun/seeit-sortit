# Street Cleanliness

**Volume share:** Part of the ~25% environmental block
**Weekly volume:** Graffiti ~1,020/week, dog fouling and litter also high
**Photo classifiable:** Yes
**Time sensitive:** Low (except biohazards)
**Responsible authority:** Borough street cleaning / environmental services

## Issue Types
- Dog fouling
- Graffiti / tags
- Fly-posting / illegal posters / stickers
- Leaves / moss / weeds on pavement
- Street washing needed (spillage, stains)
- General litter accumulation

## Severity Ranking Signals
- High street / commercial area (footfall proxy from TfL station data)
- Near schools, hospitals, care homes
- Offensive or hate-related graffiti → escalate to police as well
- Biohazard (dog fouling near playground)

## Routes To
- Borough street cleaning team
- Police (hate-related graffiti)
- Property owner (graffiti on private property — council may still clean)
