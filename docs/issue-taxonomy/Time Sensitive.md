# Time Sensitive

Issues requiring urgent response. These should trigger priority routing and shorter SLA expectations in our CRM.

## Immediate (same-day response expected)
- Gas smell → [[Utilities]] (999 / National Gas Emergency)
- Exposed electricity → [[Utilities]] (UK Power Networks emergency)
- Fallen tree blocking road → [[Trees and Vegetation]]
- Dangerous building → [[Housing]] / [[Planning and Building]]
- Illegal eviction → [[Housing]]
- Drug needles in public → [[Antisocial Behaviour]]
- Flooding / blocked drains → [[Roads and Highways]]

## Urgent (24–48 hours)
- Streetlight out on main road → [[Street Lighting and Traffic]]
- Traffic light fault → [[Street Lighting and Traffic]]
- Playground equipment damage → [[Parks and Public Spaces]]
- Burst water main → [[Utilities]]
- Giant hogweed → [[Trees and Vegetation]]
- Dangerous hanging branch → [[Trees and Vegetation]]
- Debris on road → [[Roads and Highways]]

## Standard (council SLA, typically 5–28 days)
- Potholes → [[Roads and Highways]]
- Fly-tipping → [[Waste and Fly-Tipping]]
- Graffiti → [[Street Cleanliness]]
- Abandoned vehicles → [[Vehicles]]
- Most [[Parks and Public Spaces]] issues
- [[Pavements and Footways]] non-urgent damage

## Our Agent's Role
The severity ranking agent should detect time-sensitive keywords and photo features to auto-escalate. For immediate items, the agent should bypass normal routing and trigger emergency service contact where appropriate (gas, electricity).
