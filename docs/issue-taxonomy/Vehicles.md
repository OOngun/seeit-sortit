# Vehicles

**Photo classifiable:** Yes
**Time sensitive:** No (except blocking access)
**Responsible authority:** Borough parking/environmental services, DVLA

## Issue Types
- Abandoned vehicles (cars, vans)
- Abandoned bicycles
- Abandoned hire bikes / e-scooters (Lime, Tier, etc.)
- Illegally parked vehicles
- Pavement parking
- Unnecessary engine idling
- Illegal driving on restricted roads
- Damaged EV charging points

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Blocking emergency access
- Near school (idling, illegal parking)
- Abandoned vehicle condition (hazardous fluids, fire risk)
- Duration abandoned (long-term = higher priority)

## Routes To
- Borough parking enforcement
- DVLA (vehicle registered keeper checks)
- Police (stolen/suspicious vehicles)
- Bike/scooter operator (abandoned hire vehicles)
