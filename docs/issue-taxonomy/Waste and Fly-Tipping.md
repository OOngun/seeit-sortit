# Waste and Fly-Tipping

**Volume share:** Largest environmental category, part of the ~25% environmental block
**Weekly volume:** ~1,550/week (highest single category by weekly count)
**Photo classifiable:** Yes — strong visual signal
**Time sensitive:** Moderate (health hazard, attracts more dumping)
**Responsible authority:** Borough waste/environmental services

## Issue Types
- Fly-tipping / illegal dumping (mattresses, furniture, bags)
- Overflowing public bins
- Dumped bin bags outside collection times
- Missed bin collection
- Damaged or missing bins
- Litter accumulation
- Cardboard box dumping
- Commercial waste on public land

## Why This is Our Demo Category
- Visually striking — easy for judges to understand instantly
- Emotionally resonant — people hate seeing dumped rubbish
- Geolocated — always tied to a specific spot
- Common — highest weekly volume of any single category
- Severity varies — dumped asbestos vs a bin bag, good for showing ranking

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Proximity to schools, playgrounds, hospitals
- Hazardous materials present (asbestos, chemicals, needles)
- Blocking pavement/road access
- Population density of area (Census 2021 LSOA data)
- Repeat location (FixMyStreet scraped data shows clustering)

## Routes To
- Borough waste services (primary)
- Environment Agency (hazardous waste, large-scale dumping)
- Housing association (if on their land)
