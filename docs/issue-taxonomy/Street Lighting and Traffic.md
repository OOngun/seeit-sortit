# Street Lighting and Traffic

**Volume share:** Common, part of infrastructure reports
**Photo classifiable:** Partially (dark street photo, damaged signal)
**Time sensitive:** Yes — safety critical
**Responsible authority:** Borough highways, TfL (traffic signals on major roads)

## Issue Types
- Streetlight not working
- Streetlight on during day
- Traffic light fault
- Damaged pedestrian crossing
- School crossing signal fault

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Location on high-crime area → higher priority
- Near school or hospital
- Road with collision history (STATS19)
- Pedestrian crossing vs side street light

## Routes To
- Borough highways (most streetlights)
- TfL (traffic signals on TfL-managed roads)
- UK Power Networks (if power supply issue)
