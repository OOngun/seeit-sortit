# Planning and Building

**Photo classifiable:** Yes (visible construction, extensions)
**Time sensitive:** Moderate
**Responsible authority:** Borough planning department

## Issue Types
- Unauthorised building works
- Breach of planning conditions
- Dangerous structures
- Unauthorised change of use (residential to commercial, etc.)

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Dangerous structure → immediate
- Conservation area violation → higher priority
- Listed building works → higher priority
- Scale of unauthorised works

## Routes To
- Borough planning enforcement
- Building control (dangerous structures)
- Historic England (listed buildings)
