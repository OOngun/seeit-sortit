# Noise and Nuisance

**Photo classifiable:** No — description/voice based
**Time sensitive:** Yes (often time-of-day dependent)
**Responsible authority:** Borough environmental health

## Issue Types
- Noise from neighbours (music, parties, DIY)
- Construction noise (outside permitted hours)
- Commercial noise (pubs, clubs, restaurants)
- Animal noise (barking dogs)
- Busking / street entertainment
- Burglar alarms (persistent)

## Intake Path
Voice/text description. No photo classification possible. Agent should capture:
- Type of noise
- Time of day / duration / frequency
- Exact location
- Impact on reporter

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Outside permitted construction hours (before 8am, after 6pm weekdays)
- Frequency (ongoing vs one-off)
- Residential density of area
- Near hospital or school
- Night-time noise (stricter regulations)

## Routes To
- Borough environmental health / noise team
- Police (if antisocial behaviour component)
- Licensing authority (licensed premises)
