# Food and Business

**Photo classifiable:** Limited
**Time sensitive:** Yes (food safety)
**Responsible authority:** Borough environmental health / trading standards

## Issue Types
- Food safety concerns at premises
- Licensing violations (alcohol, entertainment)
- Illegal street trading
- Health and hygiene issues at businesses

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Imminent food safety risk → urgent
- Pattern of complaints against same premises
- Food hygiene rating history

## Routes To
- Borough environmental health (food safety, hygiene)
- Borough licensing (alcohol, entertainment)
- Trading standards (illegal trading, consumer issues)
