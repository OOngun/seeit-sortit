# Pollution

**Photo classifiable:** Sometimes (smoke, discoloured water)
**Time sensitive:** Yes (health hazard)
**Responsible authority:** Borough environmental health, Environment Agency

## Issue Types
- Air quality / smoke / fumes
- Smells and odours
- Light pollution
- Water contamination
- Land contamination

## Severity Ranking Signals
- Near residential area, school, hospital
- Toxic substance involved
- Scale of contamination
- Wind direction / population downwind (for air quality)

## Routes To
- Borough environmental health
- Environment Agency (water/land contamination, industrial emissions)
- HSE (workplace related)
